Last updated: March 26, 2026

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage your account
  • To send verification codes to your email for sign-in
  • To share your location with your tour guide or guests during active tours (opt-in for guests, automatic for guides with location permission)
  • To deliver push notifications about tour messages and updates
  • To display your name and profile to guides or guests during tours
  • To enable reviews, ratings, and post-tour features

3. Location Data

Guests: TripToe collects location data to enable sharing your location with your tour guide, even when the app is closed or not in use. Location sharing is opt-in — you choose when to start and stop sharing. Location data is sent to the tour guide in real time and is not stored after the tour session ends.

Guides: TripToe collects location data to enable sharing your location with tour guests, even when the app is closed or not in use. This allows guests to see the guide on the map during active tours. Location data is not stored after the tour ends.

Both guides and guests can revoke location permission at any time in their device settings.

5. Data Security

All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS. Authentication uses JWT tokens, and verification codes are hashed before storage. Passwords are not stored — guides authenticate via Google OAuth and guests authenticate via email verification codes.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: You can view your account data in the app's Account screen.
  • Correction: You can update your name, phone number, and profile photo in the app.
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and personal data at triptoe.app/delete-account or by contacting support@triptoe.app. Upon deletion, your personal data (name, email, phone number, profile photo) will be permanently deleted. Non-identifying records (such as anonymous booking counts) may be retained for guide reporting purposes.
  • Location: You can stop sharing your location at any time and revoke location permission in your device settings.

7. Children's Privacy

TripToe is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date.
